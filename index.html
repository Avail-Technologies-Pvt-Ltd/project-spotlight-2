Our Story

BS Zai Farm has been a family-owned operation for over a decade. We believe in sustainable farming practices and treating our animals with respect and care.

Our hens roam freely on pasture, enjoying sunshine, fresh air, and a natural diet that results in products with superior flavor and nutrition.

We deliver farm-fresh products directly to your doorstep or local markets, ensuring you get the highest quality possible.
